From a prone position with the pelvic bowl is firmly contracted interiorly toward the center line of the body while the pubis is tucked under.  The legs are extended back and the tops of the feet are flat.  The palms are flat and the elbows are on the mat, stacked right below the shoulders.  On an inhalation, lift the sternum and extend the neck away from shoulders with the elbows, palms and pelvic bone firmly attached to the mat.
